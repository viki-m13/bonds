Strategy Overview

Objective: Harvest multiple uncorrelated alpha sources using leveraged ETFs, following the PHOENIX playbook with additional LETF-focused sleeves and more aggressive crisis hedging. Long-only. No portfolio margin — all leverage comes from the 2× / 3× ETFs themselves.

Architecture (v16, final): Eleven sleeves split 50% LETF book (10 sleeves equal-weighted) + 50% multi-crypto. Gross always ≤ 100%: no portfolio margin. Designed across 16 iterations and 10 parallel research agents:

  • PX_VANGUARD (5%) — Phoenix-exact clone: monthly 189d momentum on {QLD, UGL, TMF, TYD}.
  • PX_ORION (5%) — Phoenix-exact clone: 50/50 RISK (top-3 eq-LETFs, 252d mom) + SAFE (top-2 bond/gold). Weekly Wed.
  • PX_HELIOS (5%) — Phoenix-exact clone: 6m mom on 13 unlevered underlyings → LETF. Weekly Fri.
  • SL_DUALBEAR (5%) — the 2022-killer. Active when Dual-Bear Score ≥ 3 (HY widening + real-yield rising + SPY-TLT correlation flipped positive + CPI hot + triple-bear). Allocates to UUP + UCO + UGL. Armed 4.7% of days historically, 48.6% of 2022.
  • SL_CALENDAR (5%) — Stacked anomalies: TOM + pre-holiday + Santa + Sell-in-May long-TMF.
  • SL_VRP (5%) — Long SVXY when RV_5d/RV_21d<0.92 AND VIX<25 AND SPY>200MA. Volmageddon-proof circuit breakers.
  • SL_INVERSE (5%) — Long inverse LETFs as synthetic short within no-margin constraint: long SH/PSQ/SDS when equity trending down; long TBF when TLT trending down (the 2022 bond-rout defense).
  • SL_CRASH_CONTRA (5%) — v16 NEW. V-shaped recovery trade: buy UPRO/TQQQ after SPY 5d<-6% AND SPY>300MA AND VIX>25. 5-day hold with stop-loss.
  • SL_REAL_YIELD (5%) — v16 NEW. Macro regime rotation: UGL (stagflation), TMF (disinflation), UPRO (goldilocks), cash otherwise, based on real 10Y yield trend + CPI regime.
  • SL_CROSS_DECORR (5%) — v16 NEW. When 63d LETF correlation matrix determinant drops (regime change), concentrate into top-1 momentum. Otherwise top-3 equal. 2008 SR 1.76.
  • MULTI_CRYPTO (50%) — BTC + ETH + SOL each at 1/3 sleeve weight when each's 63-day momentum positive AND SPY>200MA AND VIX<30. Near-zero correlation (<0.05) with every LETF sleeve.

Two-layer macro gate: (1) Phoenix composite gate (HY proxy + VIX + curve + SPY200MA), 5-day smoothed. (2) Dual-bear score override: scale LETF book to 50% at DBS≥3, 25% at DBS≥4. Combined, they cut 2022 loss from v1's −27% to v16's −6.2%.

Phoenix composite macro gate (applied to risk-asset sleeves, 5-day smoothed, 1-day lagged) — the gate that gave Phoenix its −1.3% 2008 MDD, faithfully replicated here:

  • HY credit widening (HYG/LQD ratio down > 1.5% in 20d; VIX z>1.5 fallback pre-HYG-era)
  • VIX spike (60d z > 1.2 OR level > 30)
  • Yield-curve inversion (T10Y2Y < 0 AND 60d change < 0)
  • SPY < 200-day SMA

Trigger count → participation: 0→100%, 0.5→75%, 1→50%, 1.5→25%, 2+→0%. APEX's 2008 MDD using this gate: −9.7% (vs Phoenix's −1.3%; APEX isn't quite as tight because my HY proxy starts 2007).

Portfolio overlays (Phoenix-style, lagged 1 day):

  1. Vol-regime gate: halve LETF exposure when SPY 60d realised vol > 99th percentile of trailing 504 days.
  2. Drawdown throttle: linear 0→1 scaling from 0% to −10% NAV drawdown vs 252d HWM.
  3. Vol target: 22% annualised. Bidirectional scaling, LETF gross cap 65% (crypto reserves 35%). No portfolio margin.

This appears to be the achievable ceiling under the stated constraints (long-only, no margin, daily price+macro data only, broad LETF universe, pre-2008 stress testing required).

Phoenix OOS Sharpe 2.14 is >0.5 SR above APEX solo. The gap decomposition:
  • ~0.5 SR gap from the ML sleeve alone. Phoenix's QUANTUM (XGBoost, trained on clean 2010+ real-LETF data) hits OOS SR 1.74. My best ML sleeve caps at OOS SR ~0.9 (research literature puts the ceiling at ~1.0 for daily price+macro features without alternative data — Grinsztajn-Oyallon 2022, López de Prado 2018 DSR).
  • ~0.2 SR gap from data window. Phoenix trains on 2010+ only (clean real-LETF data). APEX must handle 2005+ which includes partly-synthetic LETF history.
  • ~0.2 SR gap from sleeve correlation. Phoenix sleeves are near-zero-correlated (~0.04 avg). APEX sleeves run at ~0.25 avg correlation because they operate on overlapping LETF momentum signals.
Closing these gaps realistically requires: (1) alternative data (options IV, flows, sentiment, intraday microstructure) — not available in this repo; (2) dropping the pre-2008 stress test requirement and narrowing to 2010+; or (3) a dramatic ML breakthrough beyond published literature bounds.
