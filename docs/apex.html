Strategy Overview

Objective: Harvest multiple uncorrelated alpha sources using leveraged ETFs, following the PHOENIX playbook with additional LETF-focused sleeves and more aggressive crisis hedging. Long-only. No portfolio margin — all leverage comes from the 2× / 3× ETFs themselves.

Architecture (v3, Phoenix-faithful): Seven sleeves — 6 LETF sleeves sharing 65% of capital (equal-weighted) + a 35% BTC sleeve. Total gross ≤ 100%: no portfolio margin. The LETF sleeves are exact clones of three Phoenix sleeves plus three orthogonal adds:

  • PX_VANGUARD — Phoenix-exact: monthly 189d momentum on {QLD, UGL, TMF, TYD}, top-2, inverse-60d-vol weighted, 200d MA filter.
  • PX_ORION — Phoenix-exact: 50/50 RISK (top-3 of 11 eq-LETFs by 252d mom, macro-gated) + SAFE (top-2 of 4 bond/gold by composite 0.7·mom + 0.3·low-vol). Weekly Wednesday rebal.
  • PX_HELIOS — Phoenix-exact: 6m momentum on 13 unlevered underlyings {SPY,QQQ,TLT,GLD,IEF,XLK,XLF,XLE,SMH,VNQ,EEM,FXI,USO} → LETF expression. Defensives (TLT/GLD/IEF) bypass equity gate. Weekly Friday rebal.
  • V3 SECTOR — Top-1 of {TECL,FAS,SOXL,ERX,DRN} by 63d mom, weekly, market-filtered.
  • V4 ML5 — XGBoost regressor on 34 features (5/21/63/126/252d mom, 21/63d vol+Sharpe, rel-SPY, dist-200dMA, VIX, curve, HYG/LQD credit, cross-sectional ranks). Rank-IC CV selects horizon. Top-3 picks, weekly. IS-only training.
  • V6 SHORT_MR — Short-term mean reversion: buy equity LETFs when RSI(2) < 5 AND asset > 200d MA AND SPY > 200d MA. 3-day hold.
  • CRYPTO — 63-day BTC momentum gated by SPY > 200d MA AND VIX < 30. Vol-scaled to 18% ann. Correlation with every LETF sleeve < 0.05 — real diversification.

Phoenix composite macro gate (applied to risk-asset sleeves, 5-day smoothed, 1-day lagged) — the gate that gave Phoenix its −1.3% 2008 MDD, faithfully replicated here:

  • HY credit widening (HYG/LQD ratio down > 1.5% in 20d; VIX z>1.5 fallback pre-HYG-era)
  • VIX spike (60d z > 1.2 OR level > 30)
  • Yield-curve inversion (T10Y2Y < 0 AND 60d change < 0)
  • SPY < 200-day SMA

Trigger count → participation: 0→100%, 0.5→75%, 1→50%, 1.5→25%, 2+→0%. APEX's 2008 MDD using this gate: −9.7% (vs Phoenix's −1.3%; APEX isn't quite as tight because my HY proxy starts 2007).

Portfolio overlays (Phoenix-style, lagged 1 day):

  1. Vol-regime gate: halve LETF exposure when SPY 60d realised vol > 99th percentile of trailing 504 days.
  2. Drawdown throttle: linear 0→1 scaling from 0% to −10% NAV drawdown vs 252d HWM.
  3. Vol target: 22% annualised. Bidirectional scaling, LETF gross cap 65% (crypto reserves 35%). No portfolio margin.
