Strategy Overview

Objective: Harvest multiple uncorrelated alpha sources using leveraged ETFs, following the PHOENIX playbook with additional LETF-focused sleeves and more aggressive crisis hedging. Long-only. No portfolio margin — all leverage comes from the 2× / 3× ETFs themselves.

Architecture (v14, final): Seven sleeves split across 60% LETF book + 40% BTC sleeve. Gross always ≤ 100%: no portfolio margin. LETF book is equal-weighted across 6 sleeves. Designed after 10 parallel research agents identified specific alpha sources:

  • PX_VANGUARD (20%) — Phoenix-exact clone: monthly 189d momentum on {QLD, UGL, TMF, TYD}, top-2, inverse-60d-vol weighted, 200d MA filter.
  • PX_ORION (20%) — Phoenix-exact clone: 50/50 RISK (top-3 of 11 eq-LETFs by 252d mom, macro-gated) + SAFE (top-2 of 4 bond/gold by 0.7·mom + 0.3·low-vol). Weekly Wed rebal.
  • PX_HELIOS (20%) — Phoenix-exact clone: 6m momentum on 13 unlevered underlyings → LETF expression. Defensives bypass equity gate. Weekly Fri.
  • SL_DUALBEAR (20%) — the 2022-killer. Active only when Dual-Bear Score ≥ 3 (HY widening + real-yield rising + SPY-TLT correlation flipped positive + CPI hot + USD/SPY/TLT-down triple). Allocates to UUP (USD) + UCO (oil) + UGL (gold) when armed. Armed ~5% of days historically, ~49% of 2022.
  • SL_CALENDAR (12%) — Stack of calendar anomalies: turn-of-month (last 2 + first 3 days), pre-holiday drift (2 days before major US holidays), Santa rally (Dec 15 - Jan 3), Sell-in-May (summer long TMF).
  • SL_VRP (8%) — Volatility risk premium: long SVXY when RV_5d/RV_21d < 0.92 AND VIX < 25 AND SPY>200MA AND no recent VIX spike. Hard Volmageddon-proof circuit breakers (exit on VIX+20%/day, 10d lockout). Max 25% sleeve allocation.
  • CRYPTO (40%) — 63-day BTC momentum gated by SPY > 200d MA AND VIX < 30. Vol-scaled to 18% ann. Correlation with every LETF sleeve < 0.05.

Two-layer macro gate: (1) Phoenix composite gate on risk-asset sleeves (HY proxy + VIX + curve + SPY200MA), 5-day smoothed. (2) Dual-bear score override on entire LETF book: scale down to 50% exposure when DBS≥3, 25% when DBS≥4. This second layer is what cut 2022 loss from −24% (v3) to −10% (v14).

Phoenix composite macro gate (applied to risk-asset sleeves, 5-day smoothed, 1-day lagged) — the gate that gave Phoenix its −1.3% 2008 MDD, faithfully replicated here:

  • HY credit widening (HYG/LQD ratio down > 1.5% in 20d; VIX z>1.5 fallback pre-HYG-era)
  • VIX spike (60d z > 1.2 OR level > 30)
  • Yield-curve inversion (T10Y2Y < 0 AND 60d change < 0)
  • SPY < 200-day SMA

Trigger count → participation: 0→100%, 0.5→75%, 1→50%, 1.5→25%, 2+→0%. APEX's 2008 MDD using this gate: −9.7% (vs Phoenix's −1.3%; APEX isn't quite as tight because my HY proxy starts 2007).

Portfolio overlays (Phoenix-style, lagged 1 day):

  1. Vol-regime gate: halve LETF exposure when SPY 60d realised vol > 99th percentile of trailing 504 days.
  2. Drawdown throttle: linear 0→1 scaling from 0% to −10% NAV drawdown vs 252d HWM.
  3. Vol target: 22% annualised. Bidirectional scaling, LETF gross cap 65% (crypto reserves 35%). No portfolio margin.
⚠ Honest reporting: The stated design objective was Sharpe 3+ OOS / CAGR 50%+. APEX does not meet this target.
