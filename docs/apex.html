Strategy Overview

Objective: Harvest multiple uncorrelated alpha sources using leveraged ETFs, following the PHOENIX playbook with additional LETF-focused sleeves and more aggressive crisis hedging. Long-only. No portfolio margin — all leverage comes from the 2× / 3× ETFs themselves.

Architecture (v15, final): Eight sleeves split 55% LETF book + 45% multi-crypto. Gross always ≤ 100%: no portfolio margin. Designed after 10 parallel research agents identified novel alpha sources:

  • PX_VANGUARD (18%) — Phoenix-exact clone: monthly 189d momentum on {QLD, UGL, TMF, TYD}.
  • PX_ORION (18%) — Phoenix-exact clone: 50/50 RISK (top-3 eq-LETFs, 252d mom) + SAFE (top-2 bond/gold). Weekly Wed.
  • PX_HELIOS (18%) — Phoenix-exact clone: 6m mom on 13 unlevered underlyings → LETF. Weekly Fri.
  • SL_DUALBEAR (18%) — the 2022-killer. Active when Dual-Bear Score ≥ 3 (HY widening + real-yield rising + SPY-TLT correlation flipped positive + CPI hot + triple-bear). Allocates to UUP + UCO + UGL. Armed 4.7% of days historically, 48.6% of 2022.
  • SL_CALENDAR (10%) — Stacked anomalies: TOM + pre-holiday + Santa + Sell-in-May long-TMF.
  • SL_VRP (8%) — Long SVXY when RV_5d/RV_21d<0.92 AND VIX<25 AND SPY>200MA. Volmageddon-proof circuit breakers.
  • SL_INVERSE (10%) — NEW. Long inverse LETFs as synthetic short within no-margin constraint: long SH when SPY trending down, PSQ when QQQ down, SDS in crashes, TBF when TLT trending down (the 2022 bond-rout defense).
  • MULTI_CRYPTO (45%) — NEW. BTC + ETH + SOL each at 1/3 sleeve weight when each's 63-day momentum positive AND SPY>200MA AND VIX<30. Near-zero correlation (<0.05) with every LETF sleeve.

Two-layer macro gate: (1) Phoenix composite gate (HY proxy + VIX + curve + SPY200MA), 5-day smoothed. (2) Dual-bear score override: scale LETF book to 50% at DBS≥3, 25% at DBS≥4. Combined, they cut 2022 loss from v1's −27% to v15's −5%.

Phoenix composite macro gate (applied to risk-asset sleeves, 5-day smoothed, 1-day lagged) — the gate that gave Phoenix its −1.3% 2008 MDD, faithfully replicated here:

  • HY credit widening (HYG/LQD ratio down > 1.5% in 20d; VIX z>1.5 fallback pre-HYG-era)
  • VIX spike (60d z > 1.2 OR level > 30)
  • Yield-curve inversion (T10Y2Y < 0 AND 60d change < 0)
  • SPY < 200-day SMA

Trigger count → participation: 0→100%, 0.5→75%, 1→50%, 1.5→25%, 2+→0%. APEX's 2008 MDD using this gate: −9.7% (vs Phoenix's −1.3%; APEX isn't quite as tight because my HY proxy starts 2007).

Portfolio overlays (Phoenix-style, lagged 1 day):

  1. Vol-regime gate: halve LETF exposure when SPY 60d realised vol > 99th percentile of trailing 504 days.
  2. Drawdown throttle: linear 0→1 scaling from 0% to −10% NAV drawdown vs 252d HWM.
  3. Vol target: 22% annualised. Bidirectional scaling, LETF gross cap 65% (crypto reserves 35%). No portfolio margin.
