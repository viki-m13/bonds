APEX — Multi-Sleeve Leveraged-ETF Ensemble

6-sleeve blend: 50% XGBoost ML alpha engine + 50% rule-based | Long-only, no portfolio margin | 1999-2026, 27 yrs | IS 2005-2018 / OOS 2019+ |

Strategy Overview

Objective: Harvest multiple uncorrelated alpha sources using leveraged ETFs, following the PHOENIX playbook with additional LETF-focused sleeves and more aggressive crisis hedging. Long-only. No portfolio margin — all leverage comes from the 2× / 3× ETFs themselves.

Architecture: Six sleeves, each emitting daily target weights that sum to ≤ 1. Each sleeve is vol-scaled individually to ~15% annualised before the blend:

  • ML5 (50%) — XGBoost regressor on 5/21/63/126/252d price features + VIX + curve + credit-proxy + cross-sectional ranks. Multi-horizon CV selects N; top-3 picks, weekly rebalance.
  • TREND (12%) — Per-LETF 200d MA + 20d momentum filter; inverse-vol sized; cap 33% per asset.
  • RPAR_CF (15%) — Inverse-vol risk parity UPRO/TMF/UGL with a correlation flip filter that pulls TMF out when SPY-TLT 20d correlation goes persistently positive (2022 protection).
  • PAA (8%) — Keller / Keuning Protective Asset Allocation: accelerating momentum (avg of 21/63/126d) top-2 among 12 LETFs, monthly.
  • ORION (8%) — 50/50 blend of RISK (top-3 eq-LETFs, 252d momentum, VIX+credit gates) and SAFE (top-2 bond/gold LETFs), weekly rebalance.
  • HELIOS (7%) — 6-month momentum on unlevered underlyings (SPY/QQQ/TLT/GLD/IEF); expressed via 3× LETF. Top-2, weekly.

Overlays (on blended weights, lagged 1 day):

  1. Bond-correlation flip filter: when SPY-TLT 20d correlation flips positive for 8/10 days, zero the bond LETFs.
  2. Dual-bear gate: when both SPY 20d < -2% and TLT 20d < -1%, scale book to 25%.
  3. VIX gate: VIX > 30 → 50% book; VIX > 40 → 25% book.
  4. Drawdown throttle: linear 0→1 scaling from 0% to -10% NAV drawdown vs 252d HWM.
  5. Vol target: 25% annualised, bidirectional but gross-capped at 100% (no margin).
⚠ Honest reporting: The stated design objective was Sharpe 3+ / CAGR 50%+ OOS. APEX does not meet this target. Achieved OOS (2019+) Sharpe is 0.87, CAGR 17.8%. Full-sample (1999-2026) is Sharpe 1.04, CAGR 19.9%. The in-sample period (2005-2018) shows stronger metrics (Sharpe 1.44, CAGR 32%) — the gap reflects genuine OOS degradation of the ML sleeve, not overfitting of rule-based components. Sharpe 3+ long-only no-margin on a broad LETF universe appears to be at or above the achievable frontier; PHOENIX's 2.37 Sharpe is probably close to the ceiling for this asset set without intra-day signals or alternative data. Published as the best honest result under the stated constraints; the research code and every experiment are preserved in apex/ for audit.
