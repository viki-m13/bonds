Strategy Overview

NEUTRINO is a high-CAGR, single-sleeve leveraged-ETF strategy. Backtest full-sample CAGR is 39.6% at Sharpe 1.26 over 16 years (2010-2026), with maximum drawdown −31.9%. NEUTRINO beats Phoenix v2 on CAGR in every window (FULL: 39.6% vs 38.0%; IS: 38.9% vs 38.4%; OOS: 40.4% vs 37.5%) at a substantially lower Sharpe and higher MDD — the growth-tilted alternative.

What is novel. NEUTRINO introduces FOUR genuinely new mechanics relative to anything in this codebase: (1) the Garman-Klass OHLC volatility estimator (statistically more efficient than realised close-to-close vol); (2) a two-horizon SMOOTH rate-velocity gate combining the trailing-yoy and trailing-90-day change in DGS10 into a continuous gate; (3) a stock-bond correlation regime gate applied only to the equity leg, that de-risks when the diversification regime breaks; (4) aggressive 45% target vol on TQQQ (3x Nasdaq), capped 2.0x.

No VIX-level macro gate, no HY-OAS gate, no 200-day SMA, no cross-sectional momentum, no ML. The risk machinery is entirely built from yield-curve dynamics and cross-asset correlation.

Honest disclosure. NEUTRINO does not beat Phoenix on Sharpe (1.26 vs 2.10) or MDD (−31.9% vs −20.5%). The single-sleeve Sharpe ceiling in this regime is ≈ 1.30; reaching Phoenix-level Sharpe would require a multi-sleeve ensemble with near-zero pairwise correlations — a structural property that's hard to engineer without copying Phoenix's specific construction. NEUTRINO's value is high CAGR and architectural simplicity.

How It Works

Universe3 LETFs: TQQQ (3x Nasdaq), TYD (3x 7-10y Treasury), UGL (2x gold). Plus SPY/TLT for the correlation gate.
Vol estimatorGarman-Klass on 21-day OHLC: σGK = √[mean(0.5·ln(H/L)² − (2ln2−1)·ln(C/O)²) × 252]. Uses the full intraday range — ~7× lower estimator variance than close-to-close.
Per-asset target volsTQQQ 45% (cap 2.0×), TYD 10%, UGL 10%. Weight = clip(target / σGK, 0, cap).
Rate-velocity gategaterate = clip(1 − rvyoy/2.0, 0, 1) · clip(1 − rv90d/1.5, 0, 1) where rvT = DGS10 − DGS10.shift(T). Catches 2022 (DGS10 went 1.6% → 4.2%) without using VIX/HY.
Correlation gateApplied to TQQQ only. corr60(SPY, TLT): ≤ 0 → 1.0; (0, 0.20] → 0.6; > 0.20 → 0.0. De-risks equity when stock-bond diversification breaks (e.g. 2022).
Self-throttle252d HWM drawdown, floor −25% (rarely activates).
Portfolio overlayVol-target 27% (60d realised); DD throttle 252d HWM, floor −15%.
ExecutionAll signals lagged shift(1); use info ≤ close[t−1]. Trade at open[t]; PnL uses open[t] → open[t+1]. TC 5 bps one-way on |Δw|.
